Summary: Latitude vs. Max Temperature

This page shows the correlation between the max temperature of a city and its latitude.

The curve of this plot shows that as the latitude of a city moves further away from the equator, the max temperature of a city drops. The curve has more datapoints towards that are greater than zero in latitude because more cities that are in the dataset are North of the equator rather than South.
